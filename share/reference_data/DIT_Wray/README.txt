Agard Chap.3 / HOM02

Direct numerical simulation of decaying turbulence in a triply periodic box 
  at 512^3 resolution.

Unpublished data from A.A.Wray (1997)


This README describes the data contained in a group of homogeneous
turbulence archive datasets.  Further information is contained in the
header at the beginning of each file.

Filename                  Contents
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
CB128_9.bin               128^3 truncation of 512^3 dataset: (u,v,w) in
                          physical space for decaying isotropic turbulence
                          at R_lambda=104.5

CB512.es                  3-d energy spectra at 28 times in
                          512^3 decaying isotropic turbulence

CB512.els                 1-d longitudinal energy spectra at 28 times in
                          512^3 decaying isotropic turbulence

CB512.ets                 1-d transverse energy spectra at 28 times in
                          512^3 decaying isotropic turbulence

CB512.ts                  3-d energy transfer spectra at 28 times in
                          512^3 decaying isotropic turbulence

CB512.f_t                 Total energy, enstrophy, integral-scale, and
                          velocity derivative skewness vs. time for 512^3
                          decaying isotropic turbulence


Files *.hL5      -->  longitudinal velocity differences
Files *.hL6      -->  transversal  velocity differences
Files *.hLm      -->  first three moments of the longitudinal and transversal velocity diff.


CB512_12.hL*              PDF's of velocity differences at R_lambda=70.45
                          in 512^3 decaying isotropic turbulence

CB512_14.hL*              PDF's of velocity differences at R_lambda=65.11
                          in 512^3 decaying isotropic turbulence

CB512_19.hL*              PDF's of velocity differences at R_lambda=60.41
                          in 512^3 decaying isotropic turbulence
